Category: Professional Monitors, Business Monitors:

Motherboards, often referred to as mainboards or mobos, are the central printed circuit boards in most computers and other expandable systems. They serve as the primary platform for connecting various hardware components, allowing them to communicate and work together harmoniously. Essentially, motherboards are the backbone of any computer, providing the essential connections for the CPU (Central Processing Unit), RAM (Random Access Memory), storage devices, graphics cards, and other peripherals.
